Personal Projects

Live & Deployed

MindBridge AI

Comprehensive mental health platform designed to make therapeutic practices more accessible and consistent. MindBridge serves as a bridge to an individual's own mind - helping people understand their mental state through AI chat support, mood tracking, and journaling. It's not a replacement for therapy, but reinforcement of therapeutic practices when experts aren't available 24/7.

AI-Powered Mental Health Features:

  • Intelligent Conversation Routing: Dynamic routing to therapeutic, educational, or general chat modes based on user intent
  • RAG Pipeline: Personalized AI responses using vector database with user's journals, moods, and mental health knowledge
  • Content Filtering: Input guardrails ensuring appropriate therapeutic interactions and safety
  • AI-Generated Insights: Personalized recommendations based on individual mood logs and journal entries

Comprehensive Mental Health Tools:

  • Interactive Mood Tracking: Mood logging with analytics and trend visualization
  • Structured Journaling: Full CRUD operations with search, tagging, and mood correlation
  • Dashboard Analytics: Real-time insights and mental health progress tracking

Enterprise Architecture:

  • Microservices Architecture: Spring Boot services with Spring Cloud Gateway
  • Multi-Storage Strategy: PostgreSQL with pgvector, Redis caching, and vector storage
  • Cloud Deployment: Google Kubernetes Engine with Terraform infrastructure as code
  • Knowledge Retrieval: Tavily Search API integration for domain-specific mental health knowledge
  • Secure Authentication: Keycloak OAuth2 with Google/GitHub social login
